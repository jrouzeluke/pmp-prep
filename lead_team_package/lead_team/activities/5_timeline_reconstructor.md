## What Sam Should Do (Coaching Advice)

**DO NOT:**
‚ùå Make all decisions unilaterally ("I'm about to just make all decisions myself")
- Would suppress conflict, not resolve it
- Prolongs Storming stage
- Team doesn't learn to work together
- Creates dependency

**INSTEAD:**

**1. Recognize This is Normal**
"Sam, your team is in Storming stage. This is completely normal and actually necessary for team development. Every high-performing team goes through this."

**2. Use Coaching Style**
- **Don't avoid the conflict** (would prolong it)
- **Don't force decisions** (would suppress, not resolve)
- **Facilitate constructive resolution**

**3. For the API Decision (REST vs GraphQL):**
"Bring Maria and Chris back together with structured discussion:
- Each presents their approach (20 min uninterrupted)
- Evaluate against criteria: performance needs, team skills, timeline, maintenance
- Team discussion using criteria
- Reach decision together or you decide based on criteria with clear rationale"

**4. For the Timeline Challenge:**
"Kyle challenging your timeline is actually good - he has expertise. Instead of being defensive:
- 'Kyle, you said it's impossible. Help me understand why.'
- Get specifics about concerns
- Adjust timeline if he's right, or explain constraints if you can't
- Convert challenge into collaboration"

**5. For the Coalitions:**
"Break up the Maria/Keisha vs Chris/Kyle dynamic:
- Mix them in pair/small group work
- Don't let them self-select into same groups
- Find common ground between factions
- Facilitate whole-team discussions on shared goals"

**6. Reinforce Shared Vision:**
"Remind them regularly why the project matters and what you're trying to achieve together. Connect their daily conflicts back to shared goals."

**7. Set Ground Rules for Disagreement:**
"Create team agreement on how to handle disagreements:
- Respectful discussion
- Focus on project needs, not personal preferences
- Time-boxed debates
- Structured decision process"
